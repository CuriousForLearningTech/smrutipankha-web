Website Sections

1. Hero Section – A captivating first impression with a headline, tagline, call-to-action (CTA) button, and a product image.
2. About Us – Brief company introduction, vision, mission, and values.
3. Features – A visually appealing breakdown of key product features with icons and animations.
4. How It Works – Step-by-step guide or process visualization on how the product functions.
5. Testimonials – Customer reviews and feedback to build trust.
6. Pricing (if applicable) – Subscription plans or pricing details (if relevant).
7. FAQs – Common questions with collapsible answers.
8. Contact Us – A form for user inquiries, along with company contact details.
9. Footer – Links to social media, company policies, and other important resources.
